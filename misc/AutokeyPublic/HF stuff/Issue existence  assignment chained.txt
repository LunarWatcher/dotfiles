Whether there needs to be an issue before you contribute, and whether or not you need to be assigned depends on the repo. Some repos require issues to exist, others don't. Some require assignment, others don't. To check what applies for the repo you're interested in, read CONTRIBUTING.md and the README. If you can't find any instructions, it doesn't hurt to ask before you act. See also <https://opensource.guide> and <https://hacktoberfest.com/resources>
